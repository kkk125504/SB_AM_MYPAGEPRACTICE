### 게시물 상세보기에 작성자 닉네임 표시(쿼리문 오류 수정)

**ArticleRepository.xml**

```java
	<select id="getArticle" resultType="Article">
		SELECT A.* , M.nickname AS extra__writer 
		FROM article AS A
		INNER JOIN `member` AS M
		ON A.memberId = M.id
		WHERE A.id = #{id}
	</select>
```

- WHERE A.id = #{id} 에서 ```A.id```부분 수정
